## Overview
This dataset contains emails labeled as either **ham (legitimate)** or **spam (junk email)**.

- **Columns**  
  - `label`: Label ham, or spam 
  - `text`: content of the email sample
  - `label_num`: 0 for ham 1 for spam


NOTE: 
only the text and label columns were used for data processing


Sourced: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wcukierski/enron-email-dataset
