# Spam Email Dataset

## Overview
This dataset contains emails labeled as either **ham (legitimate)** or **spam (junk email)**.

- **Columns**  
  - `text`: The content of the email  
  - `v1`: Label ham, or spam 

## Labels
- Ham: Normal, non-spam emails  
- Spam: Junk or malicious emails  


Ham Count: 4852 
Spam Count: 749
